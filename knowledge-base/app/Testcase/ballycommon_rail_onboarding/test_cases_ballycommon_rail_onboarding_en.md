## Scope

**In scope — Rail + Workwell (PAYE), 8 forms:**
- M01 Prefix / Welcome (BALLYCOMMON-RAIL) · M02 Your Details · M03 References · M04 Medical Self-Certification
- M05 Contract of Sentinel Scheme Sponsorship · M06 PPE · M07 Safety Critical Certifications
- M08 Lost & Stolen Sentinel Cards · M09 Declaration · M10 E2E (Rail + Workwell)

**Out of scope:** Rail + CWG (CIS, 3 forms); Construction sector.

**Design techniques:** EP (email, NIN, phone) · BVA (DOB age 16) · Decision Table (Medical all-NO/any-YES, Safety Critical gate) · State Transition (multi-form flow, Continue onboarding) · UI-state (label/placeholder/error verbatim from Figma).

## Test Data Conventions

- Traceable: `auto_bcrail_<module>_<TCnum>_20260602` (e.g. `auto_bcrail_details_010_20260602`).

- Sample valid NIN: `AB123456C` (format `AA999999A`).
- Each TC is independent; reset onboarding in the Pre-Condition.

## Ambiguity Notes (⚠️ to verify — see requirements doc §8)

- **AMB-01** Sentinel Number required → tested under the assumption it is required.
- **AMB-02** Reference Type labels "Personal referee"/"Employer referee" (per Figma).
- **AMB-03** (RESOLVED per Figma) Safety Critical has 2 independent triggers: (a) gate "Are you subject to any medical restrictions?" = YES → shows the "details of medical restrictions" field (required); (b) each cert = YES → shows that cert's own "[Cert] - Duration Held" field (required). The list of 16 certs is ALWAYS displayed, independent of the gate.
- **AMB-08** The final Declaration button shows "Next →" but its behaviour = Submit.
